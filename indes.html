Critical Reading & Information Analysis

Why Critical Reading Matters in Logistics

In logistics, information is everything. Contracts, regulations, market reports, technical specifications - your ability to quickly understand, analyze, and act on written information directly impacts your success and career growth.

üìñ The SQRRR Method

Survey

Skim headings, subheadings, charts, summary

Question

What do I need to know? What's the main point?

Read

Active reading with purpose

Recite

Summarize key points in your own words

Review

Check understanding, make connections

üéØ Critical Analysis Framework

Ask These Questions:

  • Source: Who wrote this? What's their expertise?
  • Purpose: Why was this written? What's the agenda?
  • Evidence: What data supports the claims?
  • Logic: Do the conclusions follow from the evidence?
  • Bias: What perspectives might be missing?
  • Currency: How recent is this information?
